Trade In My Car Online

Are you planning to trade in your car online before considering a different vehicle for your next purchase? At GLux Automotive, we’ve made the process to value your trade straightforward for any interested drivers. Yet some buyers have never completed the online car trade-in process before. As a result, we’ve decided to put together a guide to help you solve the question, “How to trade in my car online?” Read on to learn more and when you’re ready we’ll collect your vehicle and you can find your next ride.

How to Trade in My Car Online?

For those who’ve never completed an online car trade-in, you’re probably wondering what can be done? Well, for those who plan to trade in their car online, we’ve got some steps you can follow:

Once you’ve calculated your Value Your Trade estimate, you can explore our online inventory to find your next option.
If you learn that the trade-in estimate doesn’t cover the value of your next vehicle, you apply with our finance center to cover the remaining amount.
For those who want to trade in a vehicle with an existing loan balance, you might be nervous, however, our finance experts will go over everything to see if it’s a good option.
When you’re ready to make your trade-in, we’ll come get your vehicle. Just be sure to have the following: The Title, Auto Loan Payoff Information, Registration, and Key. One of our staff will inspect the car and finalize the estimate given during the trade-in process.

What Are The Benefits of Trading in a Car Online?

Trading in your car online is a great way to reduce what you’ll owe on your next vehicle. No matter what you’re considering, you’ll be able to put the amount from your old car towards the next option. This will take a sizable portion off your down payment, resulting in a lower balance to pay off down the road.
